Diablo Valley College

Diablo Valley College is throwing it's first ever hackathon! This is an event where you can hack together anything you can dream of, hopefully learning new programming skills along the way, then present your finished product at the end. There will me workshops, mentors, and other resources to help you build the coolest software possible. Join us for the first ever Diablo Valley Hackathon by dvcoders

Long Time Hacker or First Time Programmer, Come to DVHacks to Create, Develop, and Break!

Writing software is an amazing feat. Discovering new technologies and using them to build awesome apps it an adventrue. We're committed to bring hackers great tools to use as well mentor them for new skills.

We have an awesome lineup of mentors for DVHacks. Without spoiling too much, we have a mixture of UC grads, vetern programmers, professors,and fellow hackers for a linup of amazing talks and demos. Expect to learn about funtional programming, micocontrollers, Android, and Jekyll

We'll also have all your meals covered, snacks galore, and blazing wifi so you can get get your hacks done in style. Start working on ideas, go, now!

FAQ

Who can attend?

All current diablo valley students are welcome, as well as any other college student wanting to attend.

When is it?

April 24th and April 25th, schedule details can be found below. The event is not continuous, so we will be stopping on the 24th and starting in the morning of the 25th.

How much does it cost?

DVHacks is completely free to you! Nothing out of pocket, it's on us.

What should I bring?

A computer, chargers, and any hardware you want to hack on (we'll also be providing some if you don't have any).

I don't have a team, can I still come?

Absolutely, people go to hackathons without teams all the time. We'll have some time at the beginning of people to find a team.

What is the max team size?

You can have up to four people on a team.

I've never been to a hackathon before, is that okay?

That's more than ok. Hackathons are the perfect time to learn new skills, and we'll have plenty of on site mentors and workshops specifically for new hackers.
